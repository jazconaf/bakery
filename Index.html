Feeling hungry?

We are a classic French bakery offering housemade breads, pastries & seasonal tarts. Flavour comes first. And for that we take great care from where we source our ingredients. The flour is traditionally milled in Bretagne, the chocolate is Valrhona, the butter from Poitou Charentes (where some of the best French butter comes from) and the eggs British free range.

We are driven by passion and creativity. All our products are free of additives, food coloring and palm oil. With the simplest ingredients we can create endless flavors, shapes and textures.

Meet the team!

Our bakers were educated at the prestigious École Internationale de Boulangerie in France. Our bakery in London is ranked as one of the top ten bakeries in the city for its Tradition Française Baguette.
